

<h1>Welcome to Astrea Design System</h1>

<p>
  Welcome to Astrea Design System, a comprehensive collection of styles, components, patterns, and layouts designed to provide a unified and consistent user experience across all our products and projects.
</p>

<h2>Why Design System</h2>
<h4>a. Styles:</h4>
<p>
  Our design system offers a cohesive set of styles, including typography, color palettes, spacing, and visual elements. Consistent styles ensure a unified look and feel, enhancing brand identity and user recognition.
</p>

<h4>b. Components:</h4>
<p>
  Astrea provides a library of reusable UI components such as buttons, forms, cards, navigation bars, and more. These components maintain consistency in design and behavior, streamlining development and promoting efficiency.
</p>

<h4>c. Patterns:</h4>
<p>
  We've curated design patterns for common user interactions and workflows. These patterns are tested and optimized for usability, ensuring a seamless and intuitive user experience.
</p>

<h4>d. Layouts:</h4>
<p>
  Astrea offers predefined layouts and grid systems that facilitate the structuring and organization of content. These layouts maintain consistency in the arrangement of elements across different screens and devices.
</p>
